Permutation-based Variable Importance (PVI)¶

MIMUW XAI WINTER 2022/2023¶

In this homework we used brain stroke dataset from kaggle.

Ad. 0¶

I prepared the data (onehot encoding) and first trained RandomForestClassifier model.The model achieved 94,6% accuracy.

Ad. 1¶

Calculated PVI for the model. Results below:

According to PVI "age" is the most important feature.

Ad. 2¶

Calculated PVI on 3 different models: XGBClassifier, RandomForestClassifier with n_esitmantors=30, RandomForestClassifier with max_dept=2

Reasults from RandomForrestClassifier are pretty similar, so changing paramateres didn't affect PVI very much. Value for "age" nearly doubled in XGBClassifier and this is probably related to architecture of XGBoost.

Ad.3¶

A) Checking gini impurity¶

This is very surprising! avg_glucose_level have higher value than age. Also age, bmi and avg_glucose_level have similar value.

B) Checking value importance using TreeSHAP¶

According to SHAP age is the most important as well.

Ad.4¶

Unsurprisingly PVI shows "age" as the most important variable. This is although quite suspicious that "age" value is overwhelmingly big compared to other variables, especially in XGBoost model.

Order of results from SHAP is quite similar but distribution of values differ. Gini impurity gives very different results from both SHAP and PVI.

It was quite expected that these methods will differ from each other as they use different approaches.
